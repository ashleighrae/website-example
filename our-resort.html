Since 800 BC

About Us

Founded in 800 BC, Athena Resort is a luxurious 5 star resort located in the city of Athens, Greece. One of the oldest hotels in the world, it is the seventh largest. Athena Resort overlooks the Athens Riveria and its surrounding wonders. Athena Resort boasts a unique design principle: each of its fifteen guest floors inspired by Ancient Greek architecture. The resort itself has many guest facilities and amenties. The resort also offers daily bus tours that explore the scenery of Greece, and hotpots of famous Ancient Greek icons

Resort Amenities

Our resort has many amenities including: a pool, restaurant, gym and so much more! Be sure to check out all of our amenties before you stay so that you can make the most out of your time with us.

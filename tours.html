Our Tours

Experience a friendly, engaging and insightful tour of Ancient Greece! Tour the Parthenon, Old Temple of Athena, the celebrity homes of Plato and Aristotle and much more. Travel with ease in our luxurious temperature-controlled double-decker tour bus. Our resorts runs daily tours that explore the beauty of Ancient Greece. You can choose from our 3 hour General Celebrity Homes & Hotspots Tour or our 5 hour All-Inclusive Celebrity Homes & Hotspots Tour (which includes lunch). Who knows? You may even get the opportunity to see these celebrities yourself. For more information or to specify food allergies or preferences, please contact us.

About our tours & landmarks:

Each tour will spend 30 minutes or more at the temple of Athena, Perthenon and touring celebrity homes. However, the All-Inclusive Tour will tour the Acropolis of Athens and the Temple of Olypian Zeus also.
